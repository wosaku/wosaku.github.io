About Me



Hello! My name is Wilian Osaku.

I'm a student of Data Analytics at the British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT) in Vancouver, Canada. I'm an enthusiast of Machine Learning and Data Visualization. My programming skills are on Python, SQL, JavaScript, Android, and Tableau.
